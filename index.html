My Process

How I work

Developing websites quickly involves a combination of effective planning, utilizing Webflow's features efficiently, and having a good understanding of web design principles. Here's the brief of the process I follow:

01

Design Handoff

It involves providing all the necessary assets, specifications, and documentation needed to ensure that the design is accurately translated into a functional website or web application.

02

Development & Testing

I follow the Client-First guidelines and the Flow Gurus Checklist to keep the project organized and maintainable. I use Relume components extensively to speed up my development process. I build on the best practices for SEO and scalability. This includes thorough QA testing and responsiveness checking.

03

Feedback & Iteration

I send the website link to the designer and the client for review where they can simply leave visual comments on the live build for me to review. Tracking feedback all in the one place visually with MarkUp.io

04

Launch & Support

After transferring the site to the client's account, I do a final round of checks, help launch the site and continue monitoring the site for 3 days after the launch. I also offer team training and same-day support to ensure a smooth launch and handover.

FAQ

Find answers to common questions about my development services, turnaround times, and project management.

What is Webflow, and why do you use it?

Webflow is a powerful website building platform that allows for the creation of custom, responsive websites without the need for coding. It provides flexibility, scalability, and allows us to create visually stunning websites that are easy to manage for the clients.

Do you also offer Branding and Web Design services?

Yes, I also offer branding and web design services to complement the development process. I partner with some of the best designers to ensure the design matches your brand, tells your story, and resonates with target audiences.

Can you work with an existing website or do you only create new ones?

I can work with both existing websites and create new ones from scratch. Whether you need a brand new website or improvements to your current one, I've got you covered.

Can you migrate my existing website to Webflow?

Yes, I can migrate your existing website to Webflow, ensuring a smooth transition while preserving your content, design, and functionality. Whether you're coming from another platform or switching to Webflow from a custom-built site, I can handle the migration process.

Can you integrate third-party services or tools into my website?

Absolutely! I can integrate various third-party services, such as e-commerce platforms, analytics tools, CRM systems, and more, to enhance the functionality and capabilities of your website.

How long does it take to develop a website?

The timeline for website development varies depending on the complexity of the project and specific requirements. However, I strive to deliver high-quality websites efficiently, typically within a week.

Do you provide training on how to use Webflow or manage the website?

Yes, I offer training sessions to familiarize clients with using Webflow and managing their website's content. I believe in empowering our clients to take control of their online presence.

Can you help with website updates and changes after the project is completed?

Absolutely! I offer ongoing support and maintenance services to ensure that your website remains current and continues to meet your evolving needs.

How do I get started with your services?

Simply reach out to me through our contact form or email, and I'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project requirements and goals. From there, I'll develop a customized plan to bring your vision to life.

Still have questions?

If you have any further questions or require assistance, feel free to reach out to me. I'm here to help!
